About MPSEDC

M.P. State Electronics Development Corporation Ltd. (MPSEDC) is a nodal agency under the Department of Science & Technology, Government of Madhya Pradesh, responsible for driving the state's IT, electronics, and e-governance initiatives. MPSEDC facilitates milestone-based project implementation and addresses regulatory and operational challenges in state-level IT and infrastructure projects, with a focus on enhancing digital governance and enabling investments in the technology sector.
